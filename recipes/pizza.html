Pizza

This is a basic tomato-sauce Margarita pizza.

A simple, but delicious home-made pizza made with a few ingredients and lots of taste.

Ingredients

For the dough

  • Flour - 300g
  • Water - 180g
  • Yeast - 5g
  • Salt - 6g

For the toppings

  • Canned tomatoes - 1 can
  • Oregano - 1 pinch
  • Powdered garlic - 1 pinch
  • Salt - to taste
  • Olive oil - 1 tbsp
  • Cheese - 100g

Steps

  1. Put the yeast in the water and let it activate
  2. Add the salt to the water and combine
  3. Knead until relatively smooth dough and leave to rest and proof
  4. For the sauce, blend the tomatoes with all the spices and olive oil in a blender
  5. Put in a sauce-pan and cook until thickened
  6. Roll out the dough in a pizza-shape
  7. Spread the sauce on top of the pizza and add the cheese
  8. Cook in a 200 degree oven for 10-15 minutes
  9. Optional - Sprinkle some basil leaves on top
